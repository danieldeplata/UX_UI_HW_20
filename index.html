OVERVIEW

We were given the task to redesign a non-profit organization. The goal of the project was to create a responsive website design that would entice users to support the SD Zoo through donations or by purchasing tickets to the zoo.

My responsibilities: UI Designer (User Research and Interviews; UI Design)

USER RESEARCH AND FINDINGS

In this research project, we planned and conducted five 1-on-1 interviews with potential users that have visited the zoo or could potentially visit the zoo. We also surveyed a total of 41 users on the topics of donating and visiting the zoo.
